Membership Benefits

Our Chamber is committed to supporting the growth and success of businesses in our community. We believe that by working together, we can create a stronger local economy that benefits everyone. Whether you are a small business owner or part of a larger corporation, our chamber offers a variety of benefits to businesses. Some of the benefits include:

  • Discounts on fitness, travel, dining, and shows.
  • Referrals and networking opportunities.
  • Group insurance plan for small to midsize businesses, which offers health and dental insurance, among other products and services, with flexible, stable, and affordable rates.
